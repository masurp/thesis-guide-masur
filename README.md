# MA Thesis Guide — Communication Science @ VU Amsterdam

A Quarto Book providing comprehensive guidance for MA students in Communication Science at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam.

**Live site:** https://masurp.github.io/thesis-guide-masur/

## Overview

This guide covers:

- Course information, trajectory, and key deadlines
- Supervision expectations and peer feedback
- Writing the thesis plan and research proposal
- Research question, design, ethics, and data management
- Section-by-section writing guidance (introduction, theory, method, results, discussion)
- Literature search, APA citation, open science, and responsible AI use
- R for data analysis
- Submission, assessment, and graduation

Available in **English** (full content) and **Dutch** (in progress).
